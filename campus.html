    Training and placement

    Training and placement:

    Along with the online and regular classes, we handle Campus2Corp program through the direct visit to the premises of the colleges or institutions, to conduct the campus-training and placement programs and soft skill training for preparing the students to face the interviews confidently to avail ample job opportunities. Having vast exposure and tie ups with various organizations, our EA helps its students with ample placement assistance.

    Most of the students were exposed to mere theoretical learning in the colleges and universities. In order to remain agile in the current competitive environment, students have to improve their practical skills to match up the current industrial requirements. EA initiated the campus training programs to bridge the gap between the “classroom” and “real world experience”, by providing the practical and interactive sessions to the students. Our training program provides more opportunities for the students to apply knowledge into practical work, rather than concentrating on mere theoretical learning.

    Our customized training and placement program provides hands-on practical experience to the students and improves the personality and professional skills of the participants, so as to avail excellent career opportunities from various corporate training companies reputed organizations and accomplish success in their career. Polishing the areas of leadership, teamwork, communication skills, interpersonal behavior, time management, appearance, and attitude will help the individuals to excel in all aspects of their career. Through our effective, Interactive and Result driven Soft Skills Training programs, students can attune themselves with the industry requirements and face the world with confidence.
